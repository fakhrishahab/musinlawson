Who We Are

Musin & Lawson is an integrated public relations firm in Jakarta, Indonesia that delivers outstanding marketing, media, and business development campaigns to empower our clients to achieve their goals.

We combine an in-depth understanding of the Indonesian market with decades of international communication experience across industries including aviation, energy, petroleum, automotive, property, healthcare, and lifestyle.

Musin & Lawson works with ambitious clients who strive to have meaningful, powerful conversations with the right people, at the right time, using the right channels. Let us tell your story.

Engaging Us

No one understands your business as well as you do, but sometimes you need someone from the outside to make your story resonate with the world.

Musin & Lawson works with clients to deliver:

  • Special projects: Many of our long-term relationships start as short projects where we develop a strategy, deliver executive training, manage brand and web development, or deal with an immediate reputational issue. Special projects typically run for one to three months.
  • Ongoing support: We can work with you, year-in and year-out, as a trusted part of your team that supports your brand, promotes you to the media, manages your digital assets, and helps you achieve your long-term goals. Our six- to 24-month engagements for major, ongoing projects usually start with us implementing Musin & Lawson’s 360 strategy process, and continue for at least six months, depending on your needs.
